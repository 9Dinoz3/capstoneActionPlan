How is automation affecting the job market?

Every minute, another robot joins the global workforce. By the time you finish reading this page, 5 more will have started working.

83M Jobs displaced by automation
VS
69M New jobs created

That's 14 million workers replaced. But here's the twist: European workers saw employment increase by 1.3% when robots arrived. The difference? They learned to work WITH automation instead of against it.

Why This Matters to You

Whether you're planning to be a nurse, mechanic, graphic designer, or work retail, automation will change how you do your job. Even part-time work like cashier positions and warehouse jobs are being transformed right now. This isn't some distant future, companies are installing over half a million robots every single year. The workplace you enter after graduation will look nothing like the one your parents knew.
